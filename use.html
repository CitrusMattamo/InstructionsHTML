Instructions For Humidifier Usage

Home Placement Electrical Instructions Usage Refilling

Usage

1 / 8
Never place anything over moisture outlet when the unit is running.
2 / 8
NEVER drop or insert any object into any openings.
3 / 8
Use appliance only for intended household use as described in this manual. Any other use not recommended by the manufacturer may cause fire, electric shock, or injury to persons. The use of attachments not recommended or sold by the manufacturer may cause hazards.
4 / 8
Humidifier should be unplugged when not in use
5 / 8
NEVER tilt, move, or attempt to empty unit while it is operating. Shut off and unplug before removing the water tank and moving the unit
6 / 8
Excessive humidity in a room can cause water condensation on windows and some furniture. If this happens, turn the humidifier OFF
7 / 8
NEVER operate unit without water in the tank.
8 / 8
Do not move unit after it has been used until unit cools completely
